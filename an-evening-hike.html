An evening hike(let)

After a 10 hour drive it's time to stretch those legs. The owner of the little hut I am staying in overnight (overlooking one of Norway's zillions of fjords) points me to a path right by the cabin, and up I hike. It's not as convenient as hiking in the German Alps as there is quite a bit of undergrowth to juggle with, but in about half an hour I come to a little clearing with some nice views. As it is already getting late, I sensibly decide to turn around making this a short but nice little change to my driving routine.
