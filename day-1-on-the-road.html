Day 1 on the road

A brilliant first driving day. The weather isn't as bad as I feared after waking up to pouring rain. The atmosphere changes quickly from bright and sunny to dark and moody. Great for taking pictures. The going is quite slow with speed limits often at 60 km, never more than 80 km. Fortunately, the main tourist season is over and there are only very few cars on the roads, and most of them are travelling South and not like me up North. The most interesting part of the trip are the frequent tunnels, sometimes up to 5 km long. Nothing for the claustrophobic ones, they tend to be rather narrow and dark. I reach my first destination Grotli after about 360 km and a total driving time of about 6 hours. Winding down in the cozy Hotel bar with a nice Norwegian Gin & Tonic.
