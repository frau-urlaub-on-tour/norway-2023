4 Sep 2023

Leaving Lofoten

Will the ferry run or won't it? That's the big question when we get up at 3.15am. The ferry operator hasn't posted any disruptions on their website, so it's off to Moskenes at 4am. We are almost alone on the road, only accompanied by the occasional, very heavy shower. Our ferry departs on time, but the ride gets quite choppy about 30 minutes after leaving the port which makes a lot of passengers run for the paper bags. 

The town of Bodo proves to be a bit of a disappointment but, to Medha's delight we find a sports shop that sells the walking trousers she regretted not having bought the other day in Leknes. At 1.30pm it's time to bid farewell at the airport. Medha is flying back via Bergen to Frankfurt, for me it's the start of my southward journey back to Bergen where I will board the ferry on Sunday.
