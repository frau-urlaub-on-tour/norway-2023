Lofoten - Last Day

It's a windy and rainy last day here on Lofoten. But we couldn't ask for a better place to be in and still enjoy the weather unfolding outside. Frode drops in to bid farewell and shows us a "hidden gem" just around the corner of our house. I am not sure how best to describe this almost cave-like hideaway which was built over the last 11 years by a guy now 81 years old. It's up for sale together with the main house and barn which also has a holiday apartment. Anyone up to grab this special place for around 500.000 EUR? It's a real treat considering the location!

Another treat are the golden eagle and sea eagle we manage to observe flying close to our place and even sitting on a rock right outside our cabin.
