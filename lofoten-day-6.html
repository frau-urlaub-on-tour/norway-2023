Lofoten Day 6

Today is cabin moving day. Or rather the day we move from the cabin to the mansion. Frode (does his usefulness ever stop?) has given us a brilliant deal on a house right by the ocean. It's just come up on the market as a new property and apparently the owner wants the first guests to be ultra happy. And ultra happy we'll be in the next 4 days we are staying here, that's for sure. It's just out of this world beautiful, inside and outside.Â 

Despite all the "ohming" and "ahing" we manage to drag ourselves out for a hike around midday. It starts in Frode's village of Bo, climbs up steeply to a viewpoint over lake Bovatnet and continues from there to a ridge and a little summit right next to it. Medha can smell and hear moose, however, the sightings remain without luck. Non-existent as well is any other human being on this hike. It's just the two of us and stunning nature all around us.

We celebrate our arrival at this special place with a glass of bubbly on a rock near our mansion overlooking the ocean trying to spot orcas. Sadly, we also prove to be unlucky in this endeavour. And it's getting chilly outside, so we are heading back to the premises and heat up our private sauna.
