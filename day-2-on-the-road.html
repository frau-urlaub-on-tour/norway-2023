Day 2 on the road

Nothing is stopping me from getting an early start as I wake up at 3.30 am. As breakfast in my hotel doesn't start before 7.30 am, however, it's finally after 8.00 am when I am heading off into what I believe will turn out to be a very wet day. Miracles can happen, it turns out, and as time moves on so does the rain move away. Not completely, but enough to be able to get out of the car every now and then without being completely drenched. Today's leg is a mere 220 km to which I end up adding another 60 as I make the mistake of not checking which route my TomTom has in store for me. Too late do I realize it's having a mind of its own taking me to my overnight stop from the East, whereas I had wanted to come in from the West via the Atlantic Ocean Road.
