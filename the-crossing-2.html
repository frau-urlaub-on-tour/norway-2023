The Crossing

Ferry leaves before departure time. That must be Scandinavian efficiency. My cabin is cozy, the bed comfy and the crossing surprisingly calm and not rocky. Waking up at 6 am when we reach our first stop Stavangar. It's still dark outside but I can sense the rain. Which proves to be true when I next wake up two hours later. Good morning Norway. And a big smiley hello to wet weather.
