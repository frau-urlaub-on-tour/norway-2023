The Route

It'll be a lot of driving from the South West of Germany up to the very North of Norway. The first stretch will take me in 580 km from Neustadt to Bremen. After a two-day break and another drive of 630 km I will reach the ferry port of Hirtshals in Denmark. From there it'll be a 16,5 hour night crossing on the car ferry to Bergen. 

The next 4 days will be spent going further up the West coast of Norway, 1.480 km in total, to reach Bodo on Friday, 25 August. Here, I will pick up Medha at the airport. Together, we will cross over to the Lofoten which we intend to explore during the following week. Hopefully not only in the rain; we'll see. The weather is known to be very unpredictable up there.
